Consolidated Data

Welcome to our extensive resource, where we've meticulously compiled a comprehensive table of various template engines that we've tested. This table serves as an invaluable tool for developers, security professionals, and researchers who are exploring the capabilities, vulnerabilities, and unique characteristics of different template engines used in web applications.

Features:

  • Detailed Information: Each entry in the tables provides essential details about the template engine, including its name, version, known vulnerabilities, and special features.
  • Ease of Comparison: Organized for your convenience, the table allows you to easily compare different template engines to determine which best fits your specific requirements or to understand their security implications.

Legend:

  • IDP: Identifying Payload - Payload that is used to identify a certain template engine or group of template engines.
  • Final IDP: Final Identifying Payload - The final payload that is used to identify a specific template engine.
  • Basic Syntax: The syntax that is default to specific template engines.

Important Notice:

The Final IDP's that are included in the list may only work in the context of the decision tree as there may be some overlap with other template engines.
