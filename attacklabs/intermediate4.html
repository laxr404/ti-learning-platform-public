Spoiler

1. Paste the Polyglot: <%'${{/#@}}%>{{ input box.

2. Based on Polyglot Manipulation, you are dealing with ${…} syntax.

3. Try ${7*7} for confirmation.

4. Follow the decision tree.

5. Paste ${str('text').upper()} to confirm entering the branch.

6. Continue until you reach: ${str('text').upper()} This will not be interpreted which within this branch means we are dealing with Mako.

7. You have now confirmed the template engine is Mako.
