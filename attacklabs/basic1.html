Goal:

Hints

Hint 1

Take a look at your URL.

Hint 2

URL's usually encode their special characters. If you are stuck, try this: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Percent-encoding

Solution

Spoiler

Step 1: Paste the Polyglot: <%'${{/#@}}%>{{ in the URL after stockMessage=.

Step 2: An error message will appear that will show you that there is an encoding error.

Step 3: Using Polyglot Manipulation will identify #{} as the used syntax while # has to be encoded to %23.

Step 3.5: When %23{... or %23{@} is being injected, an error message will appear that will show you that Pug is the template engine that is being used.

Step 5: Use #{7*7} to identify whether Pug Default or Inline is being used

Step 6: Confirm Pug Inline with: #{new Date().getFullYear()}
