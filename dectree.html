Interactive Decision Tree

Identify the Template Engine via Payloads

Welcome to the Interactive Decision Tree, a tool specifically designed to help you identify the template engine used in a web application through various payloads. This decision tree is an intuitive, click-based guide that leads you through a series of choices and outcomes, ultimately helping you pinpoint the template engine in question.

How It Works:

  • Start at the Root: Begin at the root node of the tree, where you'll find the initial question or payload.
  • Choose and Click: Make a choice based on the information or results you have, and simply click on the corresponding node.
  • Navigate Through the Tree: As you make selections, the path you follow will lead you to the result.
  • Reach Your Conclusion: Continue through the tree until you reach a node that identifies the template engine.

Legend:

  • [blue dot] - Initial Node: Start here.
  • [gray dot] - Decision Node: Click these to progress based on your findings.
  • [orange dot] - Jump Node: Indicates a jump to a paired node.
  • [red or green dot] - Leaf Node: Indicates an identified template engine or the end of that path.

This tool is designed to be a hands-on guide for both beginners and experienced users. Just click through the nodes and uncover the insights you need!
